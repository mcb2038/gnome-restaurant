About

Created back in 1940 in Berlin, Germany to provide a safe heaven for Jewish people escaping the Nazis. This restaurant became a symbol of hope and freedom until the fall of the Nazi Party.

After the fall of Berlin by the Zamorakian invaders of 1952, the Gnome Stronghold was created to keep invaders at bay and to protect the Jewish population once again. This restaurant has remained a symbol for all of Germany and Gnome-kind and will continue to for many decades to come.
